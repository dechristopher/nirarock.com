Maintenance schedule
(Scout Stevenson)
January
Annual planning: Plan priorities and projects for the coming year.     fonr
March
Orchard pruning: Prepare fruit trees for upcoming season.     fonr
Plan material deliveries: Order plants, mulch, compost, corn gluten, and any other needed materials.     fonr
April: first half
Grass rehabilitation: Aerate soil near pathways. Loosen soil and overseed where needed.     fonr
Graffiti removal (quarterly): Identify any new marks and get them removed.     wc
April: second half
Irrigation: Turn on water and set up hoses, water bags, etc. Make plans for watering any new plantings or drought-stressed plants.     wc
Spring clean-up: Remove all trash. Rake the gravel path, all grass on the orchard level, and along the fenceline. Rake and clean up the Arcola entrance.     fonr
Weeding: Weed blueberries, fragrant sumac, yellowroot, ferns, berry patches, shrubs, and orchard trees.     fonr
Mulching: Mulch orchard trees, blueberries, yellowroot, and all shrubs.     fonr
May: first half
Remove invasives: Garlic mustard, bittersweet, Norway Maple sprouts.     fonr
Mowing: Cut orchard grass and paths in the meadow. Cut all orchard grass until garlic mustard stops seeding.     fonr
Clear the fenceline: Remove all climbing vines from fence.     fonr
