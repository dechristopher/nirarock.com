Directions to Nira Rock
Biking or driving: From Route 9
  1. Take Route 9 to South Huntington Avenue.
  2. After approximately 1/2 mile, make a left onto Evergreen Street.
  3. At the end of Evergreen Street, make a left onto Day Street.
  4. After about three blocks, take a left onto Nira Avenue.
  5. Go to the end of Nira Avenue. Nira Rock will be directly in front of you. Welcome!
