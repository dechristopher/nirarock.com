Directions to Nira Rock
Nira Rock has excellent access via public transportation. The Green Line (E train/Heath Street) is the most convenient, though the Orange Line is also nearby. It's also a short bike ride from almost anywhere in Boston.
To Google directions, use the address 18 Nira Avenue, Jamaica Plain, MA.
